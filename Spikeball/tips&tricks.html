Tips & Tricks

Setting up your net

    1. Take the five leg and rim pieces and conntect them to make a circle
    2. Connect the net to the rim, do this by connecting the net at 5 points equally spaced around the rim, then from there attatch the rest of the net to fill in the gaps
    3. To check if you setup the net correctly, drop a ball on it from eye level and if it bounces above your waist then you are good

practice with other people just passing and spiking the ball
