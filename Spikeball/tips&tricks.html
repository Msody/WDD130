Tips & Tricks

    Setting up your net:
  1. Take the five leg and rim pieces and connect them to make a circle
  2. Connect the net to the rim, do this by connecting the net at 5 points equally spaced around the rim, then from there attatch the rest of the net to fill in the gaps
  3. To check if you setup the net correctly, drop a ball on it from eye level and if it bounces above your waist then you are good
    Tips for playing:
  1. It is the best to play on grass so that when you dive for the ball, you reduce the risk of injury
  2. Communicate with your teamate!
  3. Practice spiking the ball from various distances and with various amounts of force to get more comfortable with how the ball reacts when you hit it
  4. pratice, pratice, pratice!
