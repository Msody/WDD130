Spikeball

America's Next Biggest Sport

Get Started

Some say that if volleyball and foursquare had a baby, that it would be called spikeball. What started out as just a game to play with my friends in the summer quickly turned into something more, I have developed a passion for this game and I want to share this passion with more people

Playing Spikeball outdoors in warm weather with 3 other people is the most optimal way to play Spikeball,gives you the best experience in my opinion, but what I love about the game is that you can play anywhere! All you need really is a net one other person.
